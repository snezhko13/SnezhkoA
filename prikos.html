About us

Frisson is a streetwear brand originally from Kiev, which originates in 2018. Created by a couple of young people whose views on street fashion and the desire to create something that has not yet been matched by 100%. We are not inspired by banal images and boring ideas. Music, art-house films, tattoos, street fashion and culture, parties, city nightlife, protests, youth and audacity - all this is intertwined and reflected in our clothes. She is "not for everyone" and this is her main feature. In each created thing we put a lot of work, meaning and promise - to be outside the gray society. Our main advantage is a thoughtful, original design and high quality clothing production.
